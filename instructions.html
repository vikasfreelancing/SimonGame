Play Game

This game is designed to evaluate one's focus level. Higher the level you cross, higher your focus level is.

How to play ?

1.) On starting the game, one of the dots will blink. Pay attention and click on the colored dot as soon as it blinks.
2.) On clicking the right dot, it takes you to next level where again a dot is blinked but this time you need to remember the pattern.
3.) On reaching next level, you have to click on the dots in the order in which they appeared starting from the very first level.

Example

Level 0

Blue dot blinked, click on the blue dot.

Level 1

Red dot blinked, click on blue dot and then red dot.

Level 2

Blue dot blinked, click on blue dot, then red dot and again blue dot.

Level 3

Yellow dot blinked, click on blue dot, then red dot, then blue dot, then yellow dot.

And So On
